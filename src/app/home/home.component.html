About Vinayaka Chavitihi

Vinayaka Chavitihi, also known as Ganesh Chaturthi, is one of the most celebrated festivals in India. It honors Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles and the patron of arts and sciences. Our community comes together every year to celebrate this auspicious occasion with great devotion and enthusiasm.
